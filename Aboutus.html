Introduction to our school

About Us

Welcome to THPT chuyên Lê Hồng Phong, one of the most prestigious high schools in Ho Chi Minh City. Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can excel academically, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens.

Our History

Founded in 1927, our school has a rich history of academic excellence and a tradition of fostering talent. Over the years, we have produced numerous successful alumni who have made significant contributions to various fields.

Our Vision

We aim to be a leading educational institution that inspires and empowers students to achieve their full potential. Our vision is to cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of the future.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and transparency.
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating everyone with dignity.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and embracing new ideas.
  • Community: Fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration.
